# Public Demo Shot List

1. Show the CoreS3 hardware, SD card, and USB-C flashing setup.
2. Flash the firmware and show the compile/upload command.
3. Boot the device and show the local AP name.
4. Join the AP from a phone and open `/app`.
5. Show the capture quality meter on a blank wall or paper.
6. Show a clear centered object generating a wild pet.
7. Capture the pet and show it in the backpack.
8. Open the encyclopedia and show owned/missing variants.
9. Show SD storage/audio manifest status.
10. If two boards are available, show MATCH, CLASH, result, and replay log.

Do not show private Wi-Fi credentials, API keys, private photos, or unlicensed
media.
